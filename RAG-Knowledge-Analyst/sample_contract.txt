LEGAL CONTRACT AGREEMENT

This Agreement is made on January 15, 2024, between ABC Corporation ("Company") and XYZ Services LLC ("Vendor").

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
The Vendor shall provide consulting services to the Company for a period of 24 months commencing on February 1, 2024.

2. COMPENSATION
The Company shall pay the Vendor $150,000 per month for services rendered. Payment terms are net 30 days.

3. RISK ALLOCATION
The Vendor assumes all liability for any damages caused by their negligence. The Company shall indemnify the Vendor against third-party claims arising from the Company's instructions.

4. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this agreement with 90 days written notice. Early termination due to breach shall result in penalty payments of $50,000.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY
Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality of proprietary information for 5 years after termination.

6. GOVERNING LAW
This agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California.

7. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Any disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration in Los Angeles County.
