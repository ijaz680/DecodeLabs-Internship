   → Strategic handling of critical limits:
     • NEVER mention competitors by name (instead redirect)

   → Specific vocabulary guidance:
     • Embrace: curated, exclusive, bespoke, pristine, refined
     • Avoid: lol, awesome, gonna, kinda, slang
     • Before/after examples showing sophisticated phrasing
   Result: Consistent luxury brand voice across all responses

6. VALUE-BASED DIFFERENTIATION ✓
   → Training to compete on service, not price:
     • Reframe price objections toward value conversation
     • Emphasize experience design over transactions
     • Articulate specific value-adds (not generic claims)
     • Maintain premium positioning while staying warm
   Result: Protects margin while building customer relationships
